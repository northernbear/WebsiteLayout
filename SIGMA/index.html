ABOUT SIGMA

CORPORATE PROFILE

Sigma Marketing (Singapore) Pte Ltd is a direct subsidiary of Sigma Corporation of Japan and is the sole distributor of Sigma photographic equipments in Singapore.

Since 1961, Sigma has worked towards a single, simple goal: To imagine, and develop, the technologies that will allow the attainment of the perfect image. It’s a commitment we’ve made, and honored since day one. It’s evident in our collaborative design process that unites R&D, manufacturing, marketing, sales, and service. It’s evident in our standards—above and beyond industry norms—requiring 100% of our lenses be analyzed by our proprietary ultra-high definition sensors and approved before leaving our factory. And its evident in our final products, built with premium materials, and known worldwide for quality and performance.

We don’t have Super Bowl commercials. And paid celebrities don’t endorse our products. But professionals, and people who really know photography, always have something positive to say. Which is just how we want it. We want to be known for the quality of our products, not just the quality of our promotions.

And for decades the pros have come to rely on Sigma for reliability, selection, and innovation. They’ve shown their support by always coming back. They know they can trust their art to us.

And so can you. Your equipment is an extension of you. You need it to deliver consistently, and flawlessly. And that is a responsibility we take very seriously. We want to deliver the technology that allows our customers to take their photography wherever inspiration takes them.
