WHAT WE DO ?

Dendeng House is manufacturer of Dendeng Chicken (Ayam), Dendeng Beef (Daging), Dendeng Sausage, Dendeng Pizza, Chicken Floss, Beef Floss, Salai Beef, Salai Chicken. Dendeng supplier to local major halal restaurants like: food caterers, bakeries, food outlets, etc. Local delivery and export enquiries are available in Frozen Packed Dendeng.
