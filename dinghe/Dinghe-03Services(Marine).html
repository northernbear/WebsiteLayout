DING HE MARINE PTE. LTD.

Ding He Marine Pte. Ltd. may be a new addition to the marine industry in Singapore but it is our attitude that sets us apart. We know that solutions are more important than questions and that the best approach is the kind of resourceful diligence which simply gets the job done.

At Ding He Marine, we treat our customers’ businesses like our own and pride ourselves at providing personalized, reliable and safest services based on their needs. To help nurture relationships, we measure our performance – and the bar is set at challenging heights.

We aim to be the pace setter in today’s dynamic and ever-growing maritime industry.

Services

In providing these services, nothing is more important to us than the well-being of people and the integrity of the goods and equipment with which we are entrusted.

IN-PORT LIMIT (IPL) SERVICES

We provide the following services to various anchorages, jetty and islands around Singapore:
  • Passenger launch services
  • Cargo conveyance
  • Spare / stores deliveries
  • Crew transportation

We operate out of Marina South Pier (MSP), mainly for operations to eastern anchorages, and West Coast Pier (WCP), mainly for operations to western anchorages and different parts of the islands and terminals.

OUT-OF-PORT (OPL) SERVICES

Our OPL boats are able to accommodate up to 12 passenger and hold up to 2 – 4 pallets at the aft cargo deck.

These flag-carrying boats are catered for vessels passing through or anchored at the Eastern and Western anchorages of Singapore. The services provided include:
  • Crew changes
  • Provision of supplies and resupplies
  • Crew manning / clearance / permits
  • Crew transportation
