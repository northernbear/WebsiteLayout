DING HE TRADING PTE. LTD.

Ding He Trading Pte. Ltd. is a trading and wholesale company based in Singapore, specializing in the wholesale and distribution of alcoholic beverages and tobacco products including OEM brands cigarettes.

We are an independent company acting as buyers and suppliers in the open market and guarantee the authenticity of all the products we carry.

We take pride at being a trustworthy and reliable company and believe in offering our customers competitive pricing from our vast selections of products.

Products

We stock and check all our products in our bonded warehouses in Singapore, Indonesia and Vietnam to ensure that we are always able to supply the right products at all times.

Our quick and responsive team ensures that our products are then shipped to our customers across Asia.
